About Me

I am a seasoned software architect with a background in artificial intelligence and machine learning. With over 23 years of experience in the tech industry, I have designed and implemented complex systems for fortune 500 companies and passionate about building scalable, reliable software solutions. My expertise spans cloud computing, microservices, and distributed systems, and I am a strong advocate for clean code and agile methodologies. I have contributed to numerous open-source projects and am deeply committed to knowledge sharing, actively engaging with the broader AI community. My dedication drives ongoing advancements in the field as I consistently seek to elevate development practices through innovative, technology-driven solutions. My influence is marked by a passion for empowering others and advancing the frontiers of AI.

Education

I hold a Master’s degree in Computer Science from Madras University and a Bachelor’s in Mathematics from Bharathidasan University.

These academic experiences provided me with a robust foundation in both the theoretical and practical aspects of computer science and information systems. This background has empowered me to approach complex challenges in the tech industry with analytical rigor and innovative solutions.
